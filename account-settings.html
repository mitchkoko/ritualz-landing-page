Your account settings allow you to manage your profile, preferences, and data. If you wish to delete your account, please follow the instructions below.

How to Delete Your Account

To delete your account and all associated data, please follow one of the methods below:

  1. Open the Ritualz app.
  2. Go to the Settings page.
  3. Select Delete My Account and confirm the action.
